# Ship of the Line — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 16, 2026**
**Last Updated: June 5, 2026**

The developer **GM2 Soft** ("Developer") values the privacy of users of the game **Ship of the Line** (the "Game") and complies with the applicable laws of the regions where the Game is released and serviced, including the Republic of Korea's *Personal Information Protection Act* and *Network Act*, the European Union's *General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)*, the United States' *California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA)*, and Japan's *Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI)*.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Developer collects, how it is used, stored, and protected, and what rights users may exercise.

The Game is serviced through the Apple App Store and Valve Corporation's Steam (PC) in the Republic of Korea, the United States, Japan, and the European region.

---

## 1. Children Under the Age of 14

**The Game is intended only for users aged 14 or older and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14.**

- The Game does not target children under 13 under the U.S. *Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)*, children under 14 under the laws of the Republic of Korea, or children below the age of digital consent (13–16, as set by each member state) under the EU GDPR.
- If it is confirmed that a child under the age of 14 has used the Game, the Developer will delete the relevant account and associated data without delay.
- Guardians who discover that a child under 14 has used the Game should notify the contact in Section 8 below.

## 2. Personal Information Collected

The Developer collects only the minimum information necessary to provide the Game.

### 2-1. Information provided by the user or collected automatically

| Category | Items Collected | When Collected |
|---|---|---|
| Guest Account | Device/platform identifier (iOS Identifier for Vendor, Steam ID, etc.), user-entered nickname | At guest login |
| Apple Account Sign-In | The unique user identifier (sub) provided by Apple, email address (if provided by the user — may be an Apple private email relay address), user-entered nickname | At Apple sign-in |
| Authentication | Login session token | At login |
| Access Information | IP address, access date and time, device/OS/game version information | At server connection |

### 2-2. Information generated and collected during gameplay

| Category | Items Collected |
|---|---|
| Game Records | Number of plays, win/loss/draw records, number of enemy ships sunk (by rate/class), number of ships lost, last play date |
| Multiplayer | Room creation/join/spectate records, match results |
| Chat | Lobby chat message content and sender nickname |
| Gameplay Logs | Game progress data such as the maneuvering, firing, hits, and damage of AI-controlled ships during Solo Play; scenario and start time of solo sessions |
| Usage Analytics (Firebase Analytics) | Screen-view and game-event usage records (battle start/end, scenario selection, etc.), app instance identifier, approximate location (country/city level) derived from IP address, device/OS information |

The Game does not collect real names, resident registration numbers, contact numbers, precise (GPS) location data, contact lists, photos, microphone/camera data, **advertising identifiers (IDFA)**, or similar information.

## 3. Purposes of Collection and Use

The Developer uses the collected information only for the following purposes:

1. **Account identification and authentication** — distinguishing guest/platform accounts, maintaining login, preventing duplicate nicknames
2. **Provision of game services** — multiplayer matchmaking, room creation/join/spectating, recording and displaying records, providing profiles
3. **Game stability and prevention of misconduct** — managing server load, blocking abnormal access/cheats/abuse, limiting concurrent connections
4. **Communication between users** — providing the lobby chat feature
5. **Improvement of game quality and AI algorithms** — analyzing gameplay logs to improve the AI opponent, the fire-delegation algorithm, and game balance
6. **Responding to user inquiries and fulfilling legal obligations**

The collected information is not used beyond the above purposes. The Developer does not send advertisements to users and does not sell or provide information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Retention and Use Period

1. The Developer destroys personal information without delay once the purpose of collection is achieved or the user requests account deletion.
2. **Guest Accounts** — Guest accounts and related data with no access record for a certain period (e.g., 12 months after last access) may be automatically deleted.
3. **Platform accounts (Apple, etc.)** — retained until the user requests deletion, and destroyed without delay upon such request.
4. **Access logs / gameplay logs** — collected for game quality and AI improvement analysis; destroyed after the analysis purpose is achieved or after a certain period from collection (e.g., 24 months), or anonymized/aggregated so that individuals cannot be identified.
5. **Chat messages** — may be retained for a certain period for misconduct/dispute response and then destroyed.
6. Where applicable laws mandate retention for a certain period, the Developer retains the information for the period prescribed by such laws.

## 5. Provision to Third Parties and Outsourcing

1. As a rule, the Developer does not provide users' personal information to outside parties, except in the following cases:
   - When the user has given prior consent
   - When required by law, or when an investigative agency requests it in accordance with procedures prescribed by law
2. **Apple Inc.** — "Sign in with Apple" authentication and the sale/payment of the Game through the Apple App Store are provided by Apple, and information processing in that process is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy (https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
3. **Valve Corporation** — the sale/payment of the Game through Steam (PC) and, where used, Steam account linkage are provided by Valve, and information processing in that process is subject to Valve's Privacy Policy (https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/). The Developer does not collect or store users' payment method information on any platform.
4. The Developer uses hosting/network infrastructure to operate the game server, which is limited to technical means for data storage and transmission.
5. **Google LLC (Firebase)** — The Developer uses Firebase Analytics for usage statistics analysis, and Firebase App Check to verify genuine clients (blocking fraudulent, automated, or forged access).
   - **Firebase Analytics**: Statistical data such as screen/event usage records, an app instance identifier, and approximate location derived from IP address are processed on Google's servers. The Developer uses a build that **does not collect the advertising identifier (IDFA)**, and does not use this data for advertising, behavioral targeting, or cross-app tracking.
   - **Firebase App Check**: A device-integrity (Apple App Attest) token is verified through Google solely to block abnormal or forged client access to the game server.
   - The above processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).
6. The Developer does **not serve advertising through ad networks, collect the advertising identifier (IDFA), or perform behavioral tracking for third-party marketing purposes (i.e., "tracking" under App Tracking Transparency).** The above use of Firebase Analytics is limited to game-quality and usage-statistics analysis, not advertising.

## 7. User Rights

Users (or their legal guardians) may exercise the following rights regarding their personal information:

- **Right of access** — to request access to their personal information and its processing status
- **Right to rectification** — to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information (nicknames can be changed directly in the in-game settings)
- **Right to erasure / account deletion** — to request deletion of their personal information and account
- **Right to restrict processing / withdraw consent** — to request suspension of processing or withdrawal of consent
- **Right to data portability (for GDPR-covered users)** — to receive the information they have provided in a structured format
- **Right to object and to lodge a complaint** — to object to the processing of personal information, or to lodge a complaint with the data protection authority of their country of residence (the Personal Information Protection Commission of Korea, the supervisory authorities of EU member states, the California Attorney General's Office, etc.)

Rights may be exercised by contacting the contact in Section 8 below, and the Developer will process such requests within the period prescribed by applicable law. For guest accounts, since identity verification means other than the device identifier are limited, providing information for account identification (nickname, device identifier, etc.) may be necessary when exercising rights.

California residents have the right under the CCPA/CPRA to opt out of the sale/sharing of personal information; however, **the Developer does not sell users' personal information or share it for advertising purposes.**
